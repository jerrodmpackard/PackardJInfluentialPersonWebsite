Who is TJ Hunt?

TJ Hunt is an automotive YouTuber based out of San Diego, CA, documenting his life on topics ranging from building cars, buying cars, traveling, and driving in drifting competitions.

He began his YouTube journey in 2014 and I have been following him since 2015. When he started, he was pursuing a nursing degree at a local community college, but as his following grew and his passions changed, he dropped out of college to pursue YouTube full-time. He has since amassed over 2.2 million subscribers on YouTube and has accumulated over 705 million total views.

He is sponsored by brands such as: NOS Energy, Magnaflow, Meguiars, and Valvoline.

What Makes Him Influential?

TJ Hunt is influential to me for many reasons. The way that he lives his life is inspiring and makes me believe that anything is possible. He ends many of his videos with his mantra, "Keep Moving Forward." TJ believes that no matter what adversity you might face, everything will be okay as long as you keep moving forward. Every dream, goal, or aspiration you could ever have is attainable if you set your mind to it and put in the hard work necessary to achieve it. He often says, "The only people who fail are the ones who stop trying."

He speaks about his use of vision boards and how he spent his days envisioning his future self having great success to manifest it into reality. He believes that the mind dictates our actions, so if you can believe that something is possible, then it will be. Another quote he lives by is, "He who thinks he can and he who thinks he can't are usually both right." This again serves to explain that the mind dictates our actions. If you believe that something is possible then you will be successful. If you believe that something is impossible or you don't believe that you can achieve it, then you will never be successful.
