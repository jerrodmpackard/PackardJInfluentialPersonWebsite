TJ's Brands

Hunt & Co

TJ Hunt's first business venture was a clothing company called Hunt & Co. He started this brand in 2016 on the foundations of his mantra, "Keep Moving Forward." It stands as a physical representation of his beliefs and mentalities that anything you put your mind to is possible with enough hard work.

StreetHunter Designs

StreetHunter Designs, founded in 2019, is TJ's latest brand. Starting an aero parts supply brand to create custom body kits for cars has been a dream of his since he was young, and in teaming up with StreetFighter LA, he was able to make his dream a reality. He leverages the talents of StreetFighter and legendary automotive artist Jon Sibal to create his one of a kind aero parts and body kids for cars such as the Subaru BRZ (pictured), Chevrolet Corvette, Toyota Supra, and even the Tesla Model 3.
